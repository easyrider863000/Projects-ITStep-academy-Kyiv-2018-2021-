DB 11

THE EXEMPLARY GRAND TOURER

Standard-bearer for an all-new generation of cars, DB11 is the most powerful and efficient ‘DB’production model in Aston Martin’s history. Available as a Coupe with the 5.2-litre twin-turbocharged V12 or the 4.0-litre twin-turbocharged V8 engine, DB11 takes our grand touring heritage to unprecedented heights.

ALL THE SIGNATURES

Instantly recognizable, familiar elements have been reinterpreted. The iconic grille – amplified, celebrated. Striking features like the clamshell hood and Aston Martin Aeroblade™ elevate DB11 to automotive art.

STRONG AND ADAPTABLE

DB11 exploits its inner strength and immense performance with completely re-worked chassis, suspension, steering and electronics. Revised suspension with adaptive damping and multiple driver-selectable dynamics enrich DB11’s adaptability.
Striking the perfect balance between excitement and refinement, the handling of DB11 AMR's chassis has been refined by Aston Martin's skilled dynamics team, delivering a greater sense of connection without harming the supple ride.
