Events

  • PERFORMANCE

    The Performance course allows you to take your car on track, on an exclusive Aston Martin track experience.

    Cost: £800 (plus local tax) per driver
    • With technical support available and full hospitality to enjoy throughout this one day programme, there is no better way to experience the performance from your car in an exhilarating race circuit environment. Your day will commence with a safety briefing and some sighting laps, following which you are left to enjoy the track and develop a greater understanding of your cars handling.

    1 Day Experience
    Safety Briefing
    Sighting laps supported by lead instructor
    1 x One-to-one in car instruction session
    Scheduled session format across morning and afternoon
    Hospitality provided, including lunch and refreshments

  • PERFORMANCE+

    The Performance+ course, offers all the content of the Performance course, with the additional benefit of having your own dedicated Aston Martin instructor for the day.

    Cost: £1,800 (plus local tax) per driver
    • Our Performance+ course allows you to receive one-to-one guidance both on and off track ensuring you get the most from your experience. In addition to this, we can offer the option of a camera fitted to your vehicle to have a video of your experience to take away from the track.

    1 Day Experience
    Safety Briefing
    Your own dedicated Aston Martin instructor for the day
    Open pit lane format across morning and afternoon sessions
    Video footage of your experience (optional extra)
    Hospitality provided, including lunch and refreshments

  • AMR ACADEMY

    Our AMR Academy is designed to take the track driving capabilities of you, the driver, to the next level.

    Starting from: £3,300 (plus local tax) per driver
    • Through a specially designed programme of courses, we offer dedicated tuition from our Aston Martin instructors, who can deliver basic track driving techniques through to advanced track handling skills and detailed theoretical analysis of the cars' performance. Our Level 1 course is where all participants commence as part of the AMR Academy. It allows our instructor to establish your current level of competency and work on key areas you wish to develop.

    1.5 Day Experience
    Safety Briefing and track walk
    Track prepared Vantage, with full technical support
    One-to-one dedicated tuition from an Aston Martin instructor
    6 x track session in addition to dedicated workshop and theory content
    Performance lap with instructor to finish
    VBOX video analysis
    Post event datalogging analysis report
    Hotel accommodation and full VIP hospitality provided throughout, including lunch and refreshments
